Callaway Gardens Web Application


Technologies Used: Leaflet | CARTO


For this 2nd project in Geography 777 I was tasked with developing a mobile first web application for Callaway Gardens which is in Pine Mountain, GA. Callaway Gardens is a premiere destination in the Southeast located about 1 hour SW of Atlanta, GA. The project took approximately five weeks to build and utilized html, css, bootstrap, and a PostgreSQL database hosted on Carto. Callaway Gardens requested that the map is consistent with current branding used on their web site. The application was built around three major themes that are important to Callaway Gardens these are: Stay, Play, and Dine. Stay will feature two hotels that are located on the property along with cabins and villas that can be rented out. Play will be the biggest category featuring attractions like the lakes, discovery center, robin beach, trails and more. Dine will include seven dining options available at the gardens.
