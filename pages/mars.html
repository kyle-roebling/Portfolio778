Mars Rover Application


Technologies Used: Leaflet | Boostrap


Creators: Daniel Fourquet | Abigail Gleason | Kyle Roebling


Greeting’s from the Mars! I hope you enjoy this interactive exploration of the red planet that follows along the paths of three of NASA’s rovers: Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit. Our map was built with the primary target user being high school students and undergraduates in an introduction to astronomy course. We expect these students should be reasonably familiar with basic map functions such as zooming, panning and scrolling.
