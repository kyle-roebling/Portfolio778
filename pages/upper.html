AWARE Upper Air Analyis


Technologies Used: Python | Metpy | Pandas


Creators: Jason Holmes | Kyle Roebling


AWARE Upper Air Analysis is a collaborated project that creates upper air weather analysis maps across North America. All data is pulled from upper air sites twice a day at 12 and 00 Z. We have maps at the following levels: 925 mb, 850 mb, 700 mb, 500 mb, 300 mb, 250 mb and 200 mb.
